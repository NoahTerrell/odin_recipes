Carnitas

Gotta love when a meal makes your whole house smell as good as thise recipes does, you know you have made the right choice.

Ingredients

  • 1x 5lb bone in pork butt
  • 1x Large navel orange
  • 8x average size cloves of garlic
  • olive oil
  • orgegeno
  • Cummen
  • Salt
  • x4 Bay leaves

Steps

  1. Rub pork with a think coat of olive oil
  2. Place pork in cooker with FAT CAP UP.
  3. Libiraly apply seasonings
    • Salt
    • Oregano
    • Cummen
  4. smash garlic cloves and place on on and around the butt
  5. peal and plave the orange on and around the booty
  6. place Bay leaves around the rump
  7. Cook on low for 8 hours
  8. shread in the cooker
  9. remove pork and distribut on a cooking sheet (leave the juice in the cooker, we will use it again)
  10. Broil until the edges are nice and crispy
  11. Move pork into large serving container, and re-moisten the pork by pouring the juice from the cooker over it
