Grilled Cheese

If you dont appreciate a good grilled cheese, well...

Ingredients

  • Texas style thick cut bread
  • Gruyere cheese sliced thick
  • Butter
  • Paprika
  • cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. Slowly bring the skillet up to a medium heat
  2. Put 2 tbls of butter into skillet
  3. Once melted place both sides of the bread in the skillet and allow butter to cover them well
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each slice of bread
  5. Gentle sprinkle a like flurry of parpika over the cheese
  6. Once the cheese started to melt, or atleast wilt, flip one side of the sandwich onto the other
  7. Flip occasionaly to assure nice consistant browning on either side
