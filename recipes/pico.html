Pico de Diablo

This fresh, and devilishy spice pico with make any dish come alive

Note- This dish gits better the longer it sits. For best results prepare the day before of the morning of.

Ingredients

  • Fresh heirloom tomatos
  • Red Onion
  • Limes
  • Cilantro
  • Serrano pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Dice Tomatos into small peice
  2. Dice onion
  3. Remove seeds from the pepper and dice to a size smaller than a grain of rice
  4. chop cilantro steams and all
  5. Combine chopped/diced/sliced ingrediants
  6. juice limes
  7. mix all ingrediants well and be carful not to mush parts
  8. Salt to taste
